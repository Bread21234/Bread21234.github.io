Debates on AI: A Breakdown of the Current Position of AI

Recent breakthroughs in technology have led to a large increase in the development of Artificial Intelligence (AI). We are currently in a period of rapid growth for AI, so more people have begun to notice and take stances on it. Disputes have arisen on whether AI should be pursued and improved on. While many fear the implementation of artificial intelligence in the daily world, abandoning AI will only inhibit humanity’s growth.

It is said that mankind is “... in a golden age of progress in artificial intelligence” so it is important that we understand the risks and take artificial intelligence seriously (“We need to talk about how good A.I. is getting”, 2022). As it currently stands, AI will become a reality whether or not people want it, for the idea is simply too seductive and tempting for humanity. Therefore, the best course of action is to ensure that AI is developed ethically and in a way that most benefits the world.

The Future Draws Near: Will AI Ever be Sentient?

Years ago, AI struggled with the most rudimentary of tasks. Chatbots, perhaps one of the simplest forms of AI, could barely create a functioning conversation. Now, large language models like “...OpenAI’s GPT-3 are being used to write screenplays, compose marketing emails, and develop video games” (“We need to talk about how good A.I. is getting”, 2022). With each year, AI is improvising at an exponential rate. Additionally, fewer experts are confidently predicting “...that we have years or even decades to prepare for a wave of world-changing AI...”(“We need to talk about how good A.I. is getting”, 2022). The future is upon mankind, and it is up to society to deal with how AI will be handled.

Sentience is defined as … For AI to be truly sentient, it needs to be able to recreate the human mind. This means that rather than simply using data to complete tasks, it would need to be able to think autonomously, feel, and most importantly, learn on its own. Moreover, the machine would need to be self-aware and AI would be aware of itself and able to perceive its internal states and emotions. At the current moment, some AI can mimic human intelligence through methods like speech algorithms. However, no AI has yet to be fully sentient. Recently, some AI programs have begun to “learn” by “‘...extracting statistical patterns from large data sets…’” and adapting based on said patterns to perform tasks such as writing speeches (“A.I. is not sentient. why do people say it is?”, 2022). However, the science is not perfect and errors are frequent, and whether or not this is an example of sentience is highly subjective. Either way, this type of learning is a step in the direction of AI sentience; therefore, AI will be sentient someday in the future.

Utopia: How AI will Improve the World

Unknown to most of the world, artificial intelligence is already being utilized every day. For instance, AI is currently used in air traffic control; to determine the most efficient methods to manage and guide planes, but air traffic control is only scratching the surface of the capability of AI. Because artificial intelligence can perform arithmetic at a pace completely infeasible to humans, implementing AI into the world will greatly increase efficiency.

For instance, artificial intelligence could be used to bolster the economy. One of the main controversies of machine learning stems from the fear that the automation of joblessness will result in unemployment. While AI does indeed render some jobs obsolete and displaces labor in the short term through what is known as “creative destruction”, “...robots can never reduce the number of jobs overall…”(Newhard, 2017). Essentially, workers are only replaced by machines when it is monetarily efficient to do so, and thus replacement results in a greater output of goods at a lower cost. Then, the price of final goods is driven down “...increasing the real wages of everyone who consumes these goods…”, freeing up cash to be spent elsewhere, effectively boosting labor needs in other industries (Newhard, 2017). As a result, job opportunities are provided to previously displaced workers, so while it may seem as though computers will destroy jobs, they will simply create job opportunities elsewhere. Throughout history, automation has always led to both more jobs and higher living standards, so it is safe to predict that the same trend will occur.

In addition to bolstering the economy, AI will also benefit the world by improving medicine. Due to modern science, it has been determined that cancer is not one disease but rather hundreds of thousands of rare diseases working together. Therefore, each tumor is, at least to some extent, unique and the same treatments cannot work for each patient (Tenenbaum and Shrager, 2011). To combat this, web-based rapid-learning communities are growing to better combat cancer. In these web-based communities, AI is used to sift incredibly fast through data and plan “...thousands of complex, multistep treatment strategies that ethically balance the needs of the individual…” in an attempt to save their life (Tenebaum and Shrager, 2011). Artificial intelligence can detect patterns across multiple cases and determine the perfect, individualized treatment plan for each patient. Moreover, AI can provide transparency for doctors to check the merit and validity of the treatment as a failsafe. Cancer treatment is only a small portion of the many things AI can do for medicine. Overall, artificial intelligence can bring on an era of individualized medical treatment plans which can greatly improve the effectiveness of medicine; therefore, AI will lead humanity into a utopia.

What this Means: How AI will be Mankind's Savior

Although many attributes of AI may seem frightening such as the automation of jobs, abandoning AI would greatly hinder society. Through machine learning and artificial intelligence, humanity will be improved through its increased efficiency, bolstered economy, and medicinal growth. At the rate that AI is advancing, it is only a matter of years before AI is a reality. If executed correctly, it will propel humanity forward and revolutionize the world, creating a utopia.
